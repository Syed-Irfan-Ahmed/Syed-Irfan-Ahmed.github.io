Donations

Long-term book writing activity requires a steady stream of support and funding in order to keep the organism alive. I gratefully acknowledge the support I have received so far that has come in so many shapes and forms. Until a steady funding is available, I have to support myself by undertaking other assignments.

Financial contributions from anonymous well-wishers supporting this work (amounts < PKR 5000) can't be made at this time.

  • I need to set up a mechanism (such as ePaisa/payPal or a dedicated bank account)
  • Such info will be posted when it becomes available

Individual donors who want to contribute to this cause in the long-term can contact me directly by email and set up a meeting.

  • Minimum level for such private pledges/donations is PKR 100,000, although any other amount will also be gratefully received.
  • A donation can also be in alternate forms promoting the mission statement. Please send me an email with details.
